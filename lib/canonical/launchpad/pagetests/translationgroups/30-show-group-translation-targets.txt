
We should now see the various distro's, projects and products that the group
has been assigned as the translator for.

  >>> browser.open(
  ...     'http://translations.launchpad.dev/+groups/polyglot')
  >>> print browser.url
  http://translations.launchpad.dev/+groups/polyglot
  >>> portlet = find_portlet(browser.contents, 'This group translates for')
  >>> for link in portlet.findAll('a'):
  ...     print '%s: %s' % (link.string, link['href'])
  Ubuntu: /ubuntu
  NetApplet: /netapplet
  GNOME: /gnome
