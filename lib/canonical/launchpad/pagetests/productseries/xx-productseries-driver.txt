= Appointing a Product Series Driver =

Appoint Sample Person as the driver of Firefox 1.0 series.

  >>> browser.addHeader('Authorization', 'Basic test@canonical.com:test')
  >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/firefox/1.0')
  >>> browser.getLink('Appoint driver').click()

  >>> browser.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/firefox/1.0/+driver'
  >>> browser.title
  'Appoint the driver for Mozilla Firefox Series: 1.0'

  >>> browser.getControl('Driver').value = 'name12'
  >>> browser.getControl('Change').click()

After changing the driver, the user is redirected to the overview page where a
message inform him of the driver change.

  >>> browser.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/firefox/1.0'

  >>> for tag in find_tags_by_class(browser.contents, 'informational'):
  ...     tag.renderContents()
  'Successfully changed the driver to test@canonical.com'

Sample Person is listed as the driver of Firefox 1.0.

  >>> sp = u'Sample Person'
  >>> project_portlet = find_portlet(
  ...     browser.contents, 'Mozilla Firefox Series: 1.0')
  >>> sp in extract_text(project_portlet.find(text='Drivers:').findNext('a'))
  True
