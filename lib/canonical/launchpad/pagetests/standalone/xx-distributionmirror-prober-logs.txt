
Every time we probe a mirror for its contents we keep a log of the probe.
All these logs can be seen by the mirror owner or the mirror admin of that
mirror's distribution.

    >>> browser.addHeader('Authorization', 'Basic mark@hbd.com:test')
    >>> browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+mirror/archive-mirror2/')

    >>> browser.getLink('Prober logs').click()
    >>> browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+mirror/archive-mirror2/+prober-logs'

    >>> "1 result" in browser.contents
    True


A random logged in user won't have the rights to see that page.

    >>> user_browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+mirror/archive-mirror2/')
    >>> user_browser.getLink('Content check logs')
    Traceback (most recent call last):
    ...
    LinkNotFoundError

    >>> user_browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+mirror/archive-mirror2/'
    ...     '+prober-logs')
    Traceback (most recent call last):
    ...
    Unauthorized: ...
