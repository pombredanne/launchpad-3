  It's not possible to add/edit a poll option after a poll is open or closed.
  That's only possible when the poll is not yet open.

  >>> print http(r"""
  ... GET /people/ubuntu-team/+poll/director-2004/+newoption HTTP/1.1
  ... Authorization: Basic Zm9vLmJhckBjYW5vbmljYWwuY29tOnRlc3Q=
  ... """)
  HTTP/1.1 200 Ok
  ...This poll is already closed, you can't add new options...

  >>> print http(r"""
  ... GET /people/ubuntu-team/+poll/never-closes/+newoption HTTP/1.1
  ... Authorization: Basic Zm9vLmJhckBjYW5vbmljYWwuY29tOnRlc3Q=
  ... """)
  HTTP/1.1 200 Ok
  ...This poll is already open, you can't add new options...
